Breaking his silence on the breakup. Nick Jonas commented on his split from longtime girlfriend Olivia Culpo on Friday, June 26, speaking on the relationship for the first time since the two parted ways.

"She's an amazing person," the former child star said of 23-year-old Culpo, whom he started dating in the summer of 2013. "We had a great, a beautiful two years together." 

"It's been a crazy couple months and I wish the best for her in the future," Jonas added. "It is tough, though." (The star first broke his silence to E!)
