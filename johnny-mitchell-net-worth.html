Early Life and Background

Johnny Mitchell, a name now synonymous with edgy and authentic comedy, hails from Portland, Oregon. His journey is anything but ordinary.

Initially, Mitchell’s life took a dramatic turn when he served time in the Oregon State Penitentiary for marijuana trafficking. This experience, though challenging, laid the foundation for his future in comedy.

Rise to Fame

While in prison, Mitchell discovered his knack for stand-up comedy, a skill he honed behind bars. Upon his release, he moved to Hollywood, a bold move that marked the beginning of his ascent in the world of show business.

Over a decade later, Mitchell has emerged as one of the most prominent up-and-coming comedic voices in the country.

Literary Success: Days Of The Trap

In 2020, Mitchell expanded his repertoire by publishing a novel, Days Of The Trap.
