Kashena Sampson's 'Wild Heart' Albums Gets UK Release

July 02, 2024
blog

Kashena Sampson is Nashville’s hottest new singer/songwriter and this May she will release her hit album ‘Wild Heart’ in the UK. The LP was self-released by the talented rising star back in last August in the USA. The critically acclaimed, timeless record is proof that we should never give up on our dreams.

UK fans have been able to download the ‘Wild Heart’ album since its US release last year, but with its official May 18th release in Britain it means that Sampson will be promoting the record over here for the first time, and it also means that Brit fans will be able to purchase physical copies of the LP exclusively through Rough Trade Records.

And that’s not all, along with the official UK release of her self-released LP, the multi-talented artist will also be performing live at a number of festivals and headline slots this year. On May 31st Kashena Sampson will become the latest Nashville country star to perform at the Slaughtered Lamb venue in London, and on June 1st she will be playing at Red Rooster Festival, before taking to the stage at the Long Road Festival on September 9th.

Make sure you follow Kashena Sampson on Twitter @kashenasampson to keep up to date with all her latest music and tour news!
