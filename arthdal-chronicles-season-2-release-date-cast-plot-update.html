Arthdal Chronicles, A fantasy and romantic Korean TV series premiered on tvN in 2019 before being featured on Netflix. The series has been directed by Kim Won Seok and written by Park San Hyun and Kim Young-Hyun. Season 1 streamed for 18 episodes, and the reception was quite impressive. The series is based on a mythical and fictional world of Arth in which citizens get into a fight to build a new society. Fans have been looking forward to the release of season 2, and here is all we know about a comeback of the series. 

Arthdal Chronicles series Storyline:

The series is all about building a society in the mythical land of Arth. The plot explores a warrior whose wish is to become a king, but priestess Kim Ji Won and Kim Ok Bin are also very eager to take the same throne. Things take a different turn when people discover a type of technology that makes them win over the neighboring lands. Some people argue that this drama is very similar to game of thrones.
