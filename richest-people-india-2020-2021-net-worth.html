In our topmost position in the list of top richest Indians or rather Indian billionaires at the helm of the hierarchy is the New Delhi billionaire Mukesh Ambani.

He is not just the richest Indian person but the richest man in Asia and occupies the 17th position in the list of the richest person in the world as of today. The owner of D Mart Radhakishan Damani is the second richest Indian according to Forbes’ Real-Time Billionaires Index and Bloomberg Billionaires Index. He is the founder of DMart a chain of hypermarkets in India which has 196 stores across 11 states in India.

Radhakishan Damani net worth is estimated to be $14.7 billion making him the second richest Indian.

Our third richest person in India richest list in non-other but Cyrus S. Poonawalla a son of a racehorse breeder. Cyrus Poonawalla wealth fortune has been made through his vaccine business where he controls Serum Institute of India which apparently is the world’s largest vaccine maker.
