Hot on the heels of Coachella dropping its 2016 roster, The Governors Ball has now followed suit, unleashing a massive lineup for its sixth outing, which is set to take place at Randall’s Island Park on June 3-5.

The big names here are obviously Kanye West, The Killers, M83 and Beck, but really, the whole lineup is quite strong, with representation from a number of genres of music, resulting in an eclectic and exciting group of artists.

With 60+ acts set to perform over the three-day event, this is surely going to be one of 2016’s must-attend festivals, as is the case every year. 2015 saw Drake, Florence Welch, deadmau5 and more headline what turned out to be an epic outing, and this year’s edition should be no different.

Tickets for The Governors Ball 2016 are now on sale. Check out the full lineup below and let us know if we’ll be seeing you there.
