Jade Roper and Tanner Tolbert Looked Ecstatic During Their Wedding

March 26, 2024
blog

Paradise found! Bachelor in Paradise alums Jade Roper and Tanner Tolbert couldn’t have looked more in love during their Sunday, January 24, nuptials at the St. Regis Resort Monarch Beach in Dana Point, California, sources tell Us Weekly.

According to one insider, the pair were overflowing with emotions while reciting their vows before their guests, which included fellow Bachelor and Bachelorette alums Chris Soules, Ali Fedotowsky, Andrew Firestone, Ryan and Trista Sutter, Carly Waddell, Andi Dorfman, Nick Viall, Ivana Bozilovic, Bob Guiney and Jessica Canyon. Current Bachelor Ben Higgins was also in attendance.

“Jade and Tanner were married under a gazebo covered with greenery and tree branches,” the source tells Us, adding the romantic moment was enhanced by chandeliers hanging from trees, and tons of candles and white flowers.

"Jade and Tanner looked ecstatic and so happy as they recited their vows,” another eyewitness tells Us. “Jade was totally focused on Tanner and listening to every word he said. They were both overcome with emotion and beaming. When they were officially married, all the guests cheered and hollered and they shared a long passionate kiss.”

Roper, 29, wore a lace V-neck gown during the ceremony, with her hair pulled back and pinned with a veil. Her bouquet was made of white, cream and pink roses.
